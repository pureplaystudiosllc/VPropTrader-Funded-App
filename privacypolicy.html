This Privacy Policy explains how VPROP TECHNOLOGY PTE. LTD. 1. Who We Are

VPROP TECHNOLOGY PTE. LTD. is a company incorporated in Singapore, responsible for developing and operating the App. For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@vproptrader.com.

2. App Permissions

Our App requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: To enable online features, communication, and ad serving.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check network availability.
  • Storage/Photos/Media Permissions (e.g., READ_MEDIA_IMAGES or equivalent): To allow users to save images (such as charts or screenshots) from the App to their device gallery.

These permissions are used solely for core App functionality, customer support, and saving user-generated content.

We collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Email address or other identifiers used for Google Sign-In and account registration (this information is encrypted to ensure security).
  • Photos, messages, or other content submitted via customer support chat.
  • Any other information you choose to share with us.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Device information (e.g., model, operating system version, unique device identifiers).
  • Usage data (e.g., app interactions, timestamps, in-app purchase events).
  • Approximate location (inferred from IP address or device settings, for analytics and ad personalization).
  • Advertising ID (for personalized advertising and attribution).

3.3 Information from Third Parties

We integrate several third-party SDKs to enhance App functionality, analytics, advertising, attribution, and payments. These providers may collect data as described below, governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Services (Firebase, Google Play Billing, Google Sign-In): Used for analytics, crash reporting, attribution, in-app purchases, and authentication. May collect device identifiers, usage data, and purchase information. See Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Facebook/Meta SDK: Used for advertising, audience building, and ad measurement. May collect device identifiers, advertising ID, and app interaction data for personalized ads. See Meta's Data Policy: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/.

In-app purchases are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment details; all billing information is handled securely by Google.
